Walking to school one day I came up with this idea, "How about I make a special series for Christmas this year, it won't be that bad". The KARLSON Saga

After school on a Friday, I recorded a video on a new game made by a YouTuber I watch, DaniDev! I downloaded the game and played it while recording. I edited it and uploaded it, not expecting much attention on the video. The day after, I wake up to have a notification feed flooded with subscriber and comment notifications. It was surreal to see and I thank you all so much!
